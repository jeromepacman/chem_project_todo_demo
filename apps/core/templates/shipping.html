Replacements and returns

We offer one free replacement shipping for tracked orders if the order is lost due to transit problems. Undelivered orders due to customs, tax, import duties or any other circumstances will not be reshipped. Orders to invalid addresses will not be reshipped. Untracked orders can not be reshipped under any circumstances. US orders can not be reshipped.
We do not offer returns or exchanges. All orders are final.

Limitations

We cannot guarantee same day dispatch. We ship worldwide with the exception of UK, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Iceland and Austria. Other countries may have restrictions depending on the product. Collection in person can be considered for high value orders only (>EUR 1000), please contact our support in advance.
